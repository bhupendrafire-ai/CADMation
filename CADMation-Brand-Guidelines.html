Brand Identity & Purpose

What CADMation Is

CADMation is the Intelligent Local Copilot for CATIA V5 sheet metal design — a professional-grade desktop AI companion that works entirely offline, putting engineer-grade precision directly on the engineer's workstation.

Our identity is a commitment to consistency, visibility, and reliability within the high-stakes engineering workflow. We provide the technical infrastructure — Deep Specification Tree extraction, Interactive Tagging, and Smart Chat — to transform complex CAD data into actionable design intelligence.

Interactive Tagging isn't just a feature; it is the visual bridge between human intent and AI execution. Our identity system is engineered for flexibility and instant brand recognition, ensuring the CADMation presence remains a stable, professional anchor across every touchpoint.

Clear Space, Sizing & Logo Misuse

Clear Space Rule

The minimum clear space around the logo is equal to the height of the CADMation symbol on all four sides. This breathing room prevents visual clutter and ensures the logo remains legible against complex engineering data.

Clear Space = 1 x Symbol Height on all four sides

Minimum Dimensions

Lockup TypeDigital MinimumPrint Minimum
Horizontal Lockup112px x 34px39mm x 12mm
Vertical Lockup88px x 53px31mm x 18mm
Symbol (standalone)24px x 24px8mm x 8mm

Logo Misuse — Strictly Prohibited

To maintain the technical authority of the CADMation brand, the following actions are strictly prohibited:

XChange the logo color outside of the approved palette
XOutline, apply gradients, or use pattern fills on the logo
XStretch, distort, warp, crop, or rotate the logo
XAdd drop shadows or external visual effects
XPosition the logo over off-brand colors or busy backgrounds
XOverlay the logo on CATIA viewports where geometry obscures legibility
XSeparate the symbol from the wordmark in the horizontal lockup
XAdd extra text, icons, or graphic elements inside the logo boundary
XUse unapproved background colors behind any lockup
XRecreate or redraw the logo without written approval

When in doubt: Default to monochrome (black or white) lockup on any colored background.

Section 06
Typography: Roboto FLEX

Roboto FLEX is the exclusive typeface for CADMation. It provides the variable axis necessary for technical clarity, hierarchy, and precision across all digital and print touchpoints.

Why Roboto FLEX? CATIA V5 is a dense, information-rich engineering interface. Roboto FLEX's variable weight axes (wdth, wght, opsz) allow the same font file to serve everything from 9px specification tree labels to 48px section headings, without switching font families.

Section 07
Image Style: Focus on the Important

Our imagery mirrors the precision of the CADMation plugin itself. Every image follows a Singular Focus Rule — isolation to guide the viewer's eye directly to the technical subject.

Core Principles

APPROVED

  • Show ONE primary subject per image — a spec tree tag, BOM entry, part number, or error highlight
  • Use shallow Depth of Field — blur backgrounds, keep subject razor-sharp
  • Neutral or solid backgrounds — no assembly clutter
  • High contrast — subject pops from background
  • Close-up UI panels showing Smart Chat interaction
  • Single sheet metal parts isolated on neutral backgrounds

PROHIBITED

  • Full CATIA interface screenshots as hero images — too much noise
  • Busy CAD geometry backgrounds without selective blur
  • Stock photography of unrelated engineering or office scenes
  • Low-resolution or pixelated screenshots
  • Multiple competing focal points in a single image
  • Cluttered BOM lists without color-coded status highlighting

Section 08
Tone of Voice: Precise & Purposeful

The CADMation voice is defined by the Class Utility Simple principle. Our language must be as efficient as the code it describes.

PRECISE

Every word is technically accurate. No fluff, no filler. Engineering-grade precision in every sentence.

PURPOSEFUL

Every word Educates, Entices, or Encourages. Nothing is said just to fill space.

SIMPLE

Get to the point quickly. Simple but smart is the way to go. Short sentences. Active voice.

Writing Guidelines

RuleGuideline
VoiceActive voice exclusively
Sentence lengthUnder 20 words for UI copy; 25 max for documentation
JargonUse technical terms correctly — BOM, PartNumber, STL, Specification Tree
AvoidCorporate filler: synergy, best-in-class, leveraging, cutting-edge
AbbreviationsSpell out on first use: Bill of Materials (BOM)
NumbersSpell out one-nine; use numerals for 10+
